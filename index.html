INTRODUCTION

Hello and Welcome!

I'm Omkar Deshpande - a Senior Analytics Consultant with over 8 years of experience turning complex data into clear, actionable insights.

My journey began in Pune, India, and has taken me through global firms such as American Express, Bristol Myers Squibb, Data Axle, ZS Associates, and Wells Fargo. Along the way, I’ve designed analytics and reporting solutions that empower leaders to make confident, data-driven decisions.

At Wells Fargo, I spearheaded initiatives like the Loudspeaker and Leadership dashboards - blending data storytelling, risk insights, and automation to deliver measurable business value. My expertise spans Tableau, Power BI, SQL, and Python, with a strong focus on automation and intuitive visualization.

Today, I am based in the Netherlands and working in the energy sector, continuing my mission of using data as a driver of clarity, efficiency, and innovation. I’m passionate about simplifying the complex and helping organizations unlock the full potential of their data.

I believe impactful analytics is part science, part storytelling. From engaging stakeholders to delivering dashboards, I bridge the gap between raw numbers and strategic outcomes.

Thanks for visiting - feel free to connect and explore how thoughtful analytics can bring clarity to complex challenges.
